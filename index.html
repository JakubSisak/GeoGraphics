Experience

With 22 years of hands-on GIS experience, Jakub Sisak is totally focused on delivering reliable, "geo-graphics" solutions for mining and other industries.

BIO

When not operating his business, Jakub Sisak is the Senior GIS Analyst with New Gold Inc., the Rainy River Project. He began his career as a computer programmer/ systems analyst with CCS Central Computer Services an affiliate of the Buchanan Group in Thunder Bay, Ontario, Canada.
During his time with CCS, Jakub quickly transitioned from developing and supporting business applications to customizing and building ArcGIS tools and stand-alone applications for forestry.
In September 2007, after 7 years with CCS, Jakub was hired by DST Consulting Engineers as a GIS specialist and was the inaugural GIS professional to be hired by the firm. While with DST, Jakub provided GIS support and consulting services primarily working with the Environmental group.
Jakub accepted a position with Rainy River Resources, a junior mining/exploration company, in November 2010 within the Environmental, Public Relations and the Geology/Exploration teams in their efforts to work towards the federal and provincial Environmental Assessment and approval of the Rainy River Gold Project near Fort Frances, Ontario. This work culminated with Rainy River Project becoming the first “greenfield” mining project to receive both federal and provincial Environmental Assessment approval in Ontario in over 25 years. Now with New Gold Inc., who acquired Rainy River Resources in 2013, Jakub and his team continue to advance GIS within the company.

GIS Services

Creating, managing, analyzing, and mapping all types of geospatial data. Using GIS to connect data to a map, integrating location data with all types of descriptive information thus providing a foundation for mapping and analysis used in and almost every industry as well as science. With GIS we allow users to discover and understand patterns, relationships, and geographic context. 

Geo-Visualization

Geographic visualization is at the convergence of GIS, cartography, computer graphics, 3D modelling, image processing, computer vision, computer-aided design, geospatial data processing and analysis.

Cartography

Custom maps built from client-supplied data, surveys or existing public domain geospatial data and styled in any way imaginable using traditional and modern cartographic techniques.

Photography

On location industrial, architectural and manufacturing photography, photo processing and touch up work by an award-winning landscape photographer.

Drone Site Surveys

We offer on-location surveys with smaller rotary (non-RTK) drones to complete high orthoimagery and DSM of smaller sites (up to 200 ha). Certified Drone Pilot (Remotely Operated Aircraft Systems RPAS) in Canada (Basic operations). Service includes the acquisition of data & processing of data into 3D models, orthoimagery, DSM and point cloud.

Drone Photography, Videography & Inspections

We also provide drone aerial photography services for structure and power line inspections as well as real estate aerial photography and videography.
